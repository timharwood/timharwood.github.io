    My name’s Tım, I’m a de­signer*

    For 20 years, I’ve been build­ing and lead­ing cross-func­tional teams, to help or­gan­i­sa­tions get value through de­sign—by solv­ing prob­lems of all shapes and sizes, across all types of pro­ject. I’ve worked with FTSE cor­po­ra­tions, tech start-ups, me­dia net­works, re­tail­ers, NGOs, you name it…

    I’m mo­ti­vated (on the whole) by a com­bi­na­tion of three things&#58; a pas­sion for craft; a fas­ci­na­tion with prob­lem solv­ing; and, a be­lief in the po­ten­tial to meet peo­ple’s needs through good de­sign.

    As one of the world’s busiest the­atres, the Royal Opera House en­riches peo­ple's lives through opera and bal­let. In 2016, as well as per­form­ing to au­di­ences of 1.5m in Covent Gar­den and in cin­e­mas world­wide, they worked with >30,000 peo­ple through cre­ative learn­ing pro­jects.

    In 2017, I led the de­sign of their Learn­ing Plat­form, an on­line space for a deeper en­gage­ment with new au­di­ences, par­tic­u­larly for teach­ers, bring­ing a more di­verse, broader au­di­ence to the ROH for the pur­poses of learn­ing and dis­cov­ery.
